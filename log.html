NETFUN (Network Fundamentals)

General Ignorance

  • Latency - Measured in milliseconds
  • Reliablity - Some tasks need less reliablity than other. Credit card payments need more reliablity than a confirmation email.
  • 10 Base T = 10 mbits/s = capacity of network.
  • Hub - Half duplex. Sends packets to all nodes
  • Switch - Full duplex. Only sends packets to the correct recipient.
  • Periodic signal - Follows a pattern.
  • Aperiodic signal - Doesn't follow a pattern.
  • Time = 1/frequency(Amount of time it takes to complete one signal)
  • Low frequency - higher strength, and the further it can go
  • Peer to Peer
    • Advantages - Instantly send files between peers in network
    Disadvantages - A virus can spread through the entire network and access all files
